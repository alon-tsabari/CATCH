Instructions:

Gameplay :

Catch the red box before it moves!.
Every click on the red box will exceeds your score with 5 points, clicking on the wrong box will decrease your score with 5 points.

Time :

Remember! each red box has it's limit time before it moves by it self. 3s below 50 points, 2s below 100 points and 1s for greater.

It also decrease your score with 10 points below 50 points, 15 points below 100 points and 20 points for greater.
